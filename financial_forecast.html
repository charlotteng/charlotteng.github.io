Definitions:

(1) Customers are defined by individuals who have paid the Initiation Fee of RMB 399 during the preceding 52 weeks.

(2) Active Customers are defined by Customers who have bought at least one item during the preceding 52 weeks.

Assumptions：

(1) Figures in blue colors are assumed inputs inferred our limited historical data.

(2) The Series A Funding of $5-7 million will be closed by the end of May.

(3) Active Customers/Customers will stablize at 33%, so Lifetime per Active Customer= 3 Years.

(5) We have listed our historical "Extraordinary Items", but since they are expected to occur rarely in the conceivable future, we have not included them in the modeling to reduce unnecessary complexities.
